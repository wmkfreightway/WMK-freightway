About Us

WMK Freightway Inc. is a trusted name in long-haul transportation, proudly serving clients from California to the Midwest and East Coast. We are committed to excellence, integrity, and timely delivery with a modern fleet and experienced drivers. Our company believes in customer-first logistics, providing refrigerated and dry freight solutions with precision and care.

Our Services

We offer comprehensive freight transportation services including full truckload (FTL), less-than-truckload (LTL), and dedicated lane shipments. Our refrigerated trailers ensure temperature-sensitive goods arrive safely, while our reliable dry van service is ideal for all other cargo types. WMK Freightway guarantees safe and efficient transport, powered by a dedicated team and top-tier equipment.
